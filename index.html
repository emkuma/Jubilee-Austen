A little bit about me.

I've made my home in Providence, Rhode Island, with
my small growing family. My journey into tech started with a
degree in journalism. As I started sharing my writing online, I
was fascinated with how easily I could get my voice out
there. I was hooked and wanted to learn how to build
my own site to fit my own specific needs.



That curiosity then opened a door that could never be shut.
When I learned how to build my first website, I realized
I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I
was looking for in my work. Now I've made a full switch to
front-end development, where I can use my organization
skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.
